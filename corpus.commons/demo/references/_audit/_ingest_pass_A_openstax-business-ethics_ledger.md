## High-level structure

Eleven substantive chapters plus three substantive appendices and back-matter:

- Ch 1: Why Ethics Matter (Being a Professional of Integrity; Ethics and Profitability; Multiple versus Single Ethical Standards).
- Ch 2: Ethics from Antiquity to the Present (Ancient Athens; Ancient China; Comparing Virtue Ethics East and West; Utilitarianism; Deontology; Theory of Justice).
- Ch 3: Defining and Prioritizing Stakeholders (Stakeholder Orientation; Weighing Stakeholder Claims; Ethical Decision-Making and Prioritizing; Corporate Social Responsibility).
- Ch 4: Three Special Stakeholders — Society, Environment, Government (Corporate Law and Responsibility; Sustainability; Government and Private Sector).
- Ch 5: Impact of Culture and Time (Business Ethics and Culture; Business Ethics over Time; Geography and Religion; Universal Values?).
- Ch 6: What Employers Owe Employees (Workplace Environment; Fair Wage; Organized Workforce; Privacy in the Workplace).
- Ch 7: What Employees Owe Employers (Loyalty to Company; Loyalty to Brand and Customers; Positive Work Atmosphere; Financial Integrity; Criticism and Whistleblowing).
- Ch 8: Recognizing and Respecting the Rights of All (Diversity and Inclusion; Different Abilities and Faiths; Sexual Identification and Orientation; Income Inequalities; Animal Rights).
- Ch 9: Professions under the Microscope (Entrepreneurship and Start-Up Culture; Influence of Advertising; Insurance Industry; Health Care).
- Ch 10: Changing Work Environments and Future Trends (Telecommuting; Workplace Campuses; Alternatives to Traditional Patterns; Robotics, AI).
- Ch 11: Epilogue — Why Ethics Still Matter (Evolving Environment; Committing to an Ethical View; Becoming an Ethical Professional; Making a Difference).
- Appendix A: The Lives of Ethical Philosophers (Aristotle; Bentham/Mill; Kant; Rawls).
- Appendix B: Profiles in Business Ethics — Contemporary Thought Leaders (Bane, Barra, Benioff, Bogle, Chouinard/Marcario, Hsieh, Jordan, Nooyi, Solheim).
- Appendix C: A Succinct Theory of Business Ethics (Nature of Business Ethics; Nature of the Ethical Business Leader).
- Answer Key (excluded — answer key); Index (excluded).
