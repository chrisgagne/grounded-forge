## High-level structure

20 substantive chapters, organised loosely as:

- **Foundations (Ch 1-3):** introduction to finance; corporate structure and governance; economic foundations.
- **Financial reporting (Ch 4-6):** accrual accounting; financial statements; measures of financial health (ratios).
- **Time value of money (Ch 7-9):** single-payment TVM; equal multiple-payment TVM (annuities, perpetuities, amortisation); unequal multiple-payment TVM.
- **Securities and markets (Ch 10-12):** bonds and bond valuation; stocks and stock valuation; historical performance of US markets.
- **Statistical methods (Ch 13-14):** statistical analysis in finance; regression analysis in finance.
- **Investment decisions (Ch 15-16):** how to think about investing (CAPM, risk and return); how companies think about investing (capital budgeting: NPV, IRR, payback).
- **Capital and operations (Ch 17-20):** how firms raise capital (capital structure, WACC); financial forecasting; trade credit and working capital; risk management for the financial manager.

The Preface designates Core (Ch 1, 2, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 15, 16, 17, 18) and Extension (Ch 3, 4, 13, 14, 19, 20) chapters.
