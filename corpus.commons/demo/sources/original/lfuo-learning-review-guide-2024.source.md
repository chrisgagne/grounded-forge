# Source: Learning From Unintended Outcomes and Learning Review Implementation Guide (US Forest Service, 2024)

Revised April 2024. 98-page implementation guide for the Facilitated Learning Analysis (FLA) and Learning Review (LR) processes used by the US Forest Service and partner wildland-fire agencies. Sponsored by the Risk Management Council in cooperation with the Office of Safety & Occupational Health. Authoritative copy hosted at the Wildland Fire Lessons Learned Center: http://lessons.wildfire.gov/library/facilitated-learning-analysis-fla-implementation-guide-2024.

**Lineage.** The 2024 guide is the current canonical iteration of a process that began as Paul Chamberlain's Facilitated Learning Analysis, evolved through APA/FLA and the FLA, then merged with the Learning Review tradition to form the combined FLA-LR_Guide_2020. The 2024 revision is described in its own preface as a "drastic change from older versions": restructured around three explicit foundational principles (Just Culture, Systems Thinking, Learning), refined definitions, added inbriefing and debriefing guidance for Delegating Officials, and a consolidated Appendix with deep-dive material. The 2024 version was selected for ingestion over the 2020 predecessor because it is the current production guide actively used by USFS and partner agencies, and because the 2024 restructuring makes the underlying just-culture / systems-thinking / human-factors grounding more explicit and quotable.
